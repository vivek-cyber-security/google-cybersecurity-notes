 History of Cybersecurity
Module Overview

This module explains how cybersecurity started

It shows how cyber attacks changed over time

It helps us understand why security is important today

History of Cybersecurity

Earlier computers were standalone

Internet was not widely used

Very few security measures existed

Cybersecurity was not a serious field

Past Cybersecurity Attacks

Early attacks were simple

Hackers were mostly curious people

Attacks were not done for money

Damage was limited

Examples of Old Attacks

Computer viruses

Worms

Attacks through floppy disks

Simple system corruption

Attacks in the Digital Age

Internet usage increased

Online services became common

Digital data became valuable

Cyber attacks increased rapidly

Modern Cyber Attacks

Phishing attacks

Malware infections

Ransomware attacks

DDoS attacks

Why Cyber Attacks Are Effective

Users click on fake links

Weak passwords are used

Systems are not updated

Lack of security awareness

Methods of Attack

Social engineering

Email phishing

Malicious websites

Infected software downloads

Data Breach Response

Do not panic during a breach

Stay calm and think clearly

Inform the security team

Identify the source of attack

Take proper action

What I Learned Today

Cybersecurity evolved with technology

Old attacks were simple

Modern attacks are dangerous

Human error causes many attacks

Cybersecurity is very important today
