# Types of Cyber Threats

Cyber threats are activities that try to damage, steal, or disrupt systems and data.

## Malware
Malware is harmful software designed to damage systems or steal information.

## Phishing
Phishing is an attack where fake emails or messages are used to trick users into
sharing sensitive information like passwords.

## Ransomware
Ransomware is a type of malware that locks files and demands money to unlock them.

## Real Life Example
An email pretending to be from a bank asking for login details is an example of phishing.
