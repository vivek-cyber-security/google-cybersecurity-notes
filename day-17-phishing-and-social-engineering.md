## Day 16 – Phishing, Social Engineering and Incident Handling

## Phishing Attack

- Phishing is a type of cyber attack
- It is used to steal sensitive information
- Attackers send fake emails or messages
- They try to make the user click a malicious link
- The goal is to steal passwords, OTP, bank details or personal data
- Phishing mostly targets human mistakes

## Types of Phishing

- Email Phishing
  - Fake emails pretending to be from banks or companies
  - Asks to click a link or download attachment

- Spear Phishing
  - Targeted attack on a specific person
  - Attacker researches the victim before sending message

- Whaling
  - Targets high level executives or managers
  - Big financial impact possible

- Smishing
  - Phishing through SMS messages

- Vishing
  - Phishing through voice calls

## Social Engineering

- Social engineering means manipulating people
- Instead of attacking system, attacker attacks human mind
- It is based on trust and emotions
- Very dangerous because humans are weakest link

## Types of Social Engineering

- Pretexting
  - Attacker creates fake story
  - Pretends to be trusted person

- Baiting
  - Offers something attractive
  - Like free USB or free software

- Tailgating
  - Unauthorized person follows employee into restricted area

- Scareware
  - Fake warning messages
  - Forces user to install fake security software

## Social Engineering Principles

- Authority
  - Attacker pretends to be boss or official person

- Urgency
  - Creates panic like “Account will be blocked”

- Fear
  - Threatens with serious consequences

- Trust
  - Pretends to be known person

- Scarcity
  - Says limited time offer

## Malware Types

- Virus
  - Attaches to files
  - Spreads when file runs

- Worm
  - Spreads automatically through network

- Trojan
  - Looks like normal software
  - But contains malicious code

- Ransomware
  - Locks files
  - Demands money to unlock

- Spyware
  - Secretly collects user information

## Methods of Attack

- Phishing emails
- Malicious attachments
- Infected websites
- Weak passwords
- Exploiting software vulnerabilities
- Social engineering tricks

## Handling Cyber Attack or Data Breach

- Do not panic
- Stay calm and think clearly
- Inform security team immediately
- Do not hide the incident
- Identify source of attack
- Contain the damage
- Follow incident response plan
- Document everything properly

## Important Lesson

- Humans are biggest security risk
- Awareness is very important
- Technical knowledge alone is not enough
- Staying calm during incident is very important
- Cybersecurity is both technical and psychological field
